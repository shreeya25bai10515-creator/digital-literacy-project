#Social Media Checklist – Do’s and Don’ts
Do’s ✅
- Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible.
- Think carefully before posting or sharing content.
- Keep personal information (address, phone number, ID numbers) private.
- Regularly review and update your privacy settings.
Don’ts ❌
- Don’t share passwords, OTPs, or PINs with anyone.
- Avoid accepting friend/follow requests from unknown people.
- Don’t click on suspicious links or download unknown files.
- Don’t post sensitive or private content publicly.
- Don’t engage in online arguments, trolling, or cyberbullying.
