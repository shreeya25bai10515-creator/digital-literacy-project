# Task 4 – Part B: Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

As a college student, it is important to use social media responsibly. The following checklist outlines **Do’s** and **Don’ts** to maintain a safe and positive online presence.

---

## ✅ Do’s
- [x] Use strong, unique passwords for each account.  
- [x] Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.  
- [x] Think carefully before posting or sharing content.  
- [x] Keep personal information (address, phone number, ID numbers) private.  
- [x] Regularly review and update your privacy settings.  

---

## ❌ Don’ts
- [ ] Don’t share passwords, OTPs, or PINs with anyone.  
- [ ] Avoid accepting friend/follow requests from unknown people.  
- [ ] Don’t click on suspicious links or download unknown files.  
- [ ] Don’t post sensitive or private content publicly.  
- [ ] Don’t engage in online arguments, trolling, or cyberbullying.
