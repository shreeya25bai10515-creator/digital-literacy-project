# Task 5 – Stay Safe Online Checklist

As a college student, following safe digital practices is essential to avoid cyber threats and financial fraud.

---

##  General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails, messages, or social media.
- Verify the identity of unknown callers or messages before taking action.
- Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store or App Store.
- Keep your device and apps updated regularly.

---

##  UPI / Financial Safety
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone.
- Always verify the receiver’s name before sending money.
- Do not accept or approve unknown payment requests.
- Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources.

---

##  Report Cybercrime
- Report online fraud at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Cybercrime Helpline Number: **1930**

---

> **Remember:** Staying alert and informed is the best way to protect yourself from cybercrime.
